Welcome to the Cancer Prediction Management System!

This system is designed to allow users to record their medical input data and view predictions regarding their health status. With this platform, you can: Record Medical Inputs: Enter data related to your medical features. These features include average and worst-case values such as radius, perimeter, and area. Receive Predictions: Using advanced algorithms, the system provides predictions regarding your health status. These predictions can help you gain a better understanding of your health. View Prediction History: By logging into your account, you can review your previous inputs and predictions. This feature allows you to track changes and trends related to your health over time. Sensitive Information Protection: We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your data.
